ACCSS: Ateneo Cirle of Computer Studies Students


ACCSS is the only academic club of the Computer Studies cluster at Ateneo de Davao University. Being the only club of Computer Studies for the CS, IT, and IS students, ACCSS and its members are assuring that all students from 1st year to 4th year will have a better and enjoyable years by means of giving them activities that molds them to academic excellence, social awareness and spiritual growth.
